# Musicality notes — definitions the curriculum stands on

Kept deliberately precise, because these four get confused constantly:

- **Microtiming** — individual syllables landing slightly OFF the grid,
  with a *backward* (late) bias. Wavy, lazy, human — "Dilla time",
  "behind the beat" as an expressive per-note touch. Not a setting; a feel.
- **Swing** — a *consistent* displacement of the off-beat subdivisions:
  every "and" arrives late by the same amount, grid-wide. A rule applied
  to the grid itself (the engine's swing slider is exactly this).
- **Syncopation** — rhythmic *emphasis on off-beats*: the stress lands
  between the counted beats. About WHERE emphasis goes, not about timing
  looseness at all.
- **Pocket** — locking in slightly *behind the kick and snare*, felt more
  than heard. The relaxed authority of a rapper who is never chasing the
  beat. The core of the Laid-Back module.
- **Polymeter** — repeating a phrase whose length differs from the bar's
  time signature (a 3-beat phrase over 4/4): the phrase drifts across bars
  and realigns after a cycle. **Displacement** is the simpler cousin:
  the same phrase started at a shifted position.

Beat/bar/tempo/subdivision (L0): beat = the pulse; bar = a group of beats;
tempo = beats per minute; subdivision = slicing each beat into 2/3/4.

Compound meters: 6/8 = two big beats of three pulses; 12/8 = four big
beats of three (the "triplet feel"); 7/8 = uneven groups (2+2+3).
BPM in the engine always means the BIG felt beat.

## Tabla × G-funk (the fusion bet)

G-funk's laid-back bounce is a swung, behind-the-beat groove at roughly
85–100 BPM. A tabla **theka** carries the same kind of lilt: strong/weak
strokes (dha/tin) cycling with micro-push-and-pull inside the cycle.
The engine's tabla-feel voice (low pitch-dropping "dha" on strong beats,
short high "tin" between) is built to be the bed for the Laid-Back module:
practice the late pocket over the theka pulse, and the West Coast swing
maps naturally onto the tabla lilt — especially in 12/8, where many thekas
(e.g. dadra family feels) already roll in threes.

This is the sound worth owning: Snoop's placement, Allahabad's pulse.
